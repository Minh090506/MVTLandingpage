# Campaign Brief: Vietnam Escape Tour — Lead Generation
**MyVivaTour | April–May 2026**

---

## 1. Campaign Overview

**Campaign name:** "Escape to Vietnam 2026" Lead Gen Sprint

**Summary:** A 4-week digital campaign to generate qualified booking enquiries from Australian travellers for MyVivaTour's flagship 10-Day Vietnam Escape Tour ($2,099 AUD), using a combination of Google Ads optimisation, organic social content, and email nurture.

**Primary objective:** Generate 40–60 qualified leads (form submissions + WhatsApp enquiries) within 4 weeks, at a cost per lead under $50 AUD.

**Secondary objectives:**
- Increase escape.myvivatour.com traffic by 30% vs. prior 4-week period
- Build a remarketing audience of 1,500+ visitors for future campaigns
- Collect 100+ email addresses for ongoing nurture

---

## 2. Target Audience

**Primary segment:** Australian adults aged 35–65 planning an international holiday. They are typically couples or small groups who value convenience (flights, meals, guides included) and want a culturally immersive experience without the hassle of self-planning. Household income $80K+ AUD. Located in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide metro areas.

**Pain points and motivations:**
- Overwhelmed by trip planning — want an all-inclusive, guided solution
- Price-conscious but willing to pay for quality and peace of mind
- Concerned about safety, language barriers, and logistics in Southeast Asia
- Motivated by food, culture, scenery, and bucket-list destinations (Ha Long Bay, Hoi An)
- Triggered by friends' travel stories, social media inspiration, and seasonal deals

**Where they spend time:** Google Search, Facebook, Instagram, travel forums (TripAdvisor, OzBargain travel deals), email newsletters, YouTube travel vlogs.

**Buying stage:** Consideration to decision — they know they want a Vietnam holiday and are comparing tour operators.

---

## 3. Key Messages

**Core campaign message:** "See all of Vietnam in 10 days — flights, hotels, meals and expert guides included from $2,099 AUD."

**Supporting messages:**

1. **All-inclusive value** — "One price covers everything: return flights, 4-star hotels, all meals, entrance fees, and local guides. No hidden costs." *Proof: detailed pricing breakdown on landing page showing what's included.*

2. **Local expertise** — "Designed by Vietnamese locals who know the real Vietnam — not just the tourist trail." *Proof: itinerary includes local experiences (street food tours, family homestay, craft villages) that big operators skip.*

3. **Small group, big experience** — "Travel in groups of 12–16 for a more personal, flexible experience." *Proof: contrast with competitors running 30–40 person coaches.*

4. **Trusted by Australians** — "Australian-owned support with WhatsApp access to your guide before, during, and after your trip." *Proof: testimonials from Australian travellers, WhatsApp response time.*

**Message variations by channel:**
- Google Ads: lead with price, duration, and "all-inclusive" — high-intent searchers want specifics
- Facebook/Instagram: lead with visual storytelling and FOMO — aspirational imagery of Ha Long Bay, Hoi An lanterns, street food
- Email: lead with social proof and urgency — "spots filling for June/July departures"

## 4. Channel Strategy

### 4a. Google Ads (Search) — OPTIMISE EXISTING CAMPAIGN
**Why:** Campaign 1 ("MVT - Escape - High Intent") is already live since 7/4/2026 with 10 phrase-match keywords and 800,000 VND/day budget. This is the highest-intent channel.

**Actions this campaign:**
- Review search terms report weekly; add negative keywords aggressively
- Test 3 new ad copy variants emphasising "2026 departures" and seasonal urgency
- Add 5 new long-tail keywords from the SEO report: `[10 day vietnam tour with flights]`, `[all inclusive vietnam holiday from australia]`, `[guided vietnam tour with meals included]`, `[small group vietnam tour from australia]`, `[vietnam tour packages from australia 2026]`
- Ensure all ad extensions (sitelinks, callouts, price) are active and performing

**Budget allocation:** $1,500–$2,000 AUD (50–65% of total budget)
**Effort:** Medium

### 4b. Facebook & Instagram Ads
**Why:** Australians aged 35–65 are heavy Facebook users. Visual platform ideal for travel aspiration. Retargeting website visitors is cost-effective.

**Actions:**
- **Week 1–2:** Run a traffic/engagement campaign with 3–4 carousel ads showcasing Ha Long Bay, Hoi An, Mekong Delta, and street food. Objective: build a warm audience.
- **Week 3–4:** Retarget website visitors and engaged users with a lead-gen ad (instant form or landing page click) featuring a limited-time offer or departure urgency.
- Audience: Australians 35–65, interests in Vietnam travel, Southeast Asia, travel deals, TripAdvisor, Lonely Planet.

**Budget allocation:** $500–$700 AUD (20–25% of total budget)
**Effort:** Medium

### 4c. Organic Social (Facebook + Instagram)
**Why:** Free reach to build credibility and feed the paid funnel. Content can be repurposed across channels.

**Actions:**
- Post 3–4 times per week: mix of destination highlights, customer testimonials, itinerary sneak peeks, and "did you know" Vietnam facts
- Use Reels/short video for Ha Long Bay drone footage, street food montages
- Engage in travel Facebook groups (Australian Travel Addicts, Vietnam Travel Tips for Australians)

**Budget allocation:** $0 (organic)
**Effort:** Medium

### 4d. Email Nurture
**Why:** Convert warm leads who enquired but haven't booked. Also nurture new email signups from the landing page.

**Actions:**
- Set up a 4-email drip sequence for new leads:
  - Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + full itinerary PDF + "reply with questions"
  - Email 2 (Day 3): Social proof — 2 customer testimonials + TripAdvisor link
  - Email 3 (Day 7): FAQ answers (visa, best time to visit, what's included)
  - Email 4 (Day 14): Urgency — "June/July departures filling fast" + direct WhatsApp link
- Send weekly broadcast to full list with fresh content (destination spotlight, travel tip)

**Budget allocation:** $0–$100 AUD (email tool costs only)
**Effort:** Low–Medium

### 4e. Landing Page Optimisation
**Why:** The escape landing page is the conversion point for all traffic. Small improvements here multiply results across every channel.

**Actions:**
- A/B test hero headline (current vs. "Your 10-Day Vietnam Holiday — All-Inclusive from $2,099")
- Add a countdown timer for next departure date
- Add 1–2 new video testimonials if available
- Ensure mobile CTA bar and WhatsApp button are prominent
- Speed audit — target under 3s load time

**Budget allocation:** $0 (development time only)
**Effort:** Low
