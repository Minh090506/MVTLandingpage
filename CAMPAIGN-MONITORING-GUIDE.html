  • Xem xet chuyen doi bidding strategy (theo roadmap Phase 1→2→3)
  • Toi uu landing page dua tren GA4 data (bounce rate cao → sua content)

6. Xu Ly Tinh Huong

Neu CTR thap (< 3%)

→ Ad copy chua hap dan hoac keywords khong phu hop

  • Kiem tra headline co chua gia ($2,099), USP (All-Inclusive, Flights Included)?
  • Them cac headline moi voi urgency ("Limited Spots", "Book Now")
  • Kiem tra keyword relevance: search term co match voi ad copy?
  • Loai bo keywords rong (vd: "vietnam" qua chung chung)
  • Kiem tra ad position: neu thap → tang bid hoac cai thien Quality Score

Neu CPC qua cao (> 80,000 VND)

→ Canh tranh cao hoac Quality Score thap

  • Kiem tra Quality Score tung keyword (target > 7)
  • Cai thien Ad Relevance: dam bao ad copy chua keyword
  • Cai thien Landing Page Experience: page load nhanh, mobile-friendly
  • Thu long-tail keywords it canh tranh hon
  • Giam max CPC xuong va theo doi volume

Neu Conversion Rate thap (< 1%)

→ Landing page chua thuyat phuc hoac traffic khong dung doi tuong

  • Kiem tra GA4 Bounce Rate: > 70% = nguoi vao roi di ngay
  • Kiem tra mobile experience: form co de dien tren dien thoai?
  • Xem Search Terms: nguoi ta co thuc su tim tour Vietnam?
  • A/B test: thay doi hero section, gia, CTA button text
  • Kiem tra form co loi ky thuat? Test submit thu
  • Them urgency: countdown timer, "X spots left"

Neu Impressions thap (< 100/ngay)

→ Keywords qua hep hoac budget/bid qua thap

  • Kiem tra Impression Share: thap = can tang budget/bid
  • Mo rong keywords: them broad match modifier, them keywords moi
  • Tang budget neu Impression Share Lost (Budget) cao
  • Kiem tra location targeting: dung la Australia?
  • Tao Campaign 2 (Destination) de mo rong reach
