> Vietnamese Tech Product Discovery Platform

**URL:** https://unikorn.vn/
**Tagline:** "Khám phá sản phẩm công nghệ do người Việt xây dựng" (Discover tech products built by Vietnamese people)

---

## What is Unikorn?

Unikorn.vn is Vietnam's dedicated platform for discovering, showcasing, and supporting technology products built by Vietnamese creators. Similar to Product Hunt but focused exclusively on the Vietnamese tech ecosystem, it serves as a launchpad for local makers to share their products with the community.

### Mission

Connect Vietnamese tech creators with users who want to discover, support, and provide feedback on locally-built digital products.

## Key Features

### 1. Product Discovery
- Browse products by category
- View featured and trending products
- Filter by tags and audience type
- Search functionality

### 2. Product Listings
- Product descriptions and screenshots
- Upvote system (similar to Product Hunt)
- Rating system (5-star reviews)
- Comment/discussion threads
- Category and tag assignments

### 3. Unikorn Talks (Community Forum)
- Discussion forums for each product
- Topic-based conversations
- Feature requests and feedback
- Community Q&A

### 4. Collections (Bộ sưu tập)
- Curated product collections by theme
- Examples:
  - "Vibe Coding Tools" (5 products)
  - "Khám phá Huyền Học" - Eastern/Western mysticism apps (4 products)
  - "Ứng dụng hoàn tiền" - Cashback apps (3 products)

### 5. Creator Profiles
- Showcase multiple products
- Track follower count
- Display creator rankings

### 6. Activity Feed
- Real-time updates on new products
- Recent reviews and ratings
- Community discussions

## User Journey

### For Discoverers
1. Browse homepage for featured/trending products
2. Filter by category or tags
3. Read product descriptions and reviews
4. Upvote products they like
5. Join discussions in Talks
6. Follow creators

### For Makers
1. Register account ("Đăng nhập")
2. Submit product ("Đăng sản phẩm")
3. Requirements: MVP must be functional before submission
4. Engage with community feedback
5. Respond to Talks discussions

## Summary

Unikorn.vn fills a crucial gap in the Vietnamese tech ecosystem by providing a dedicated platform for local creators to showcase their products. With 400+ products across 8 categories and a growing community of 300+ creators, it's becoming the go-to destination for discovering Vietnamese-built technology solutions.

The platform emphasizes community engagement through its Talks feature, curated collections, and comprehensive tagging system that helps users find products relevant to their needs—whether they're students, developers, businesses, or casual users.
