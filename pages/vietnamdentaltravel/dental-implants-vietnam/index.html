Frequently Asked Questions

How much do dental implants cost in Vietnam? ▼
From $700 AUD for a single Straumann or Nobel Biocare implant, compared to $2,500+ in Australia. The cost includes the implant, abutment, and crown placement. All prices are transparent with no hidden fees.
What implant brands do you use? ▼
We use Straumann (Switzerland), Nobel Biocare (Sweden), Osstem (South Korea), and Dentium (South Korea) — the same international brands used in Australia. All implants come with manufacturer warranties.
Is it safe to get dental implants in Vietnam? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Our clinics are JCI-accredited and follow international standards. We have a 97.8% implant success rate with 10+ year longevity. Our surgeons have 10+ years experience and we use state-of-the-art 3D imaging for precision placement.
How long does the implant process take? ▼
Implant placement takes 1–3 hours. Bone integration (osseointegration) takes 3–6 months. Final crown fitting takes 2–3 weeks after integration. You can fly home 7–10 days after placement. Total timeline: 6–9 months for full completion.
Can I fly home after implant placement? ▼
Yes, you can safely fly 7–10 days after implant placement. We provide detailed post-op care instructions, pain management, and 24/7 WhatsApp support. Many patients combine their dental treatment with a vacation.
What if something goes wrong after I return to Australia? ▼
We provide lifetime remote support via WhatsApp with our surgeons. Your Australian dentist can also manage routine care. Our treatment guarantee and implant warranty cover any complications. We're always here for you.
How do I know I'll get good results? ▼
We start with a free 3D treatment plan sent within 24 hours. You approve the plan before any work begins. Plus, 4.9/5 from 100K+ verified patients means real results. Our clinics are transparent — no surprises.
Do I need a visa for Vietnam? ▼
Australian citizens can get a 90-day tourist visa on arrival, or an e-visa online. We help coordinate all logistics — airport pickup, accommodation recommendations, and post-op care arrangements.
How much should I budget for flights and hotels? ▼
Flights from Sydney to Ho Chi Minh City: ~$400–$800 AUD. 4-star hotels: $50–$100 AUD/night. Budget $1,500–$2,500 AUD total for a 2-week trip. Still a huge saving vs Australian treatment.
