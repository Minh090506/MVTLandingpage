Frequently Asked Questions

How much do dental implants cost in Vietnam?
Single implants start from $700 USD for Korean brands (Osstem, Dentium) and $1,000–1,500 for Swiss premium brands (Straumann, Nobel Biocare). All-on-4 full arch restoration starts from $4,000–7,500. These prices are all-inclusive — implant, abutment, crown, and all clinical fees. Most patients save 70–80% compared to Australian prices.
Are dental implants in Vietnam safe?
Yes. Our recommended clinics use internationally certified implant brands, follow WHO sterilization protocols, and employ dentists trained at institutions like Harvard and the University of Sydney. A 2025 study of over 158,000 implants confirmed a 97.8% survival rate — outcomes depend on brand and clinical protocol, not treatment location.
How long do I need to stay in Vietnam for dental implants?
Most implant treatments require two trips. Trip 1 (3–5 days): implant placement surgery and initial recovery. Trip 2 (3–5 days, after 3–6 months): permanent crown or bridge attachment. Some clinics offer same-day teeth-in-a-day for eligible candidates.
Do Vietnamese dentists speak English?
All clinics in our network have English-speaking dentists and dedicated international patient coordinators. Communication is clear from your first online consultation through post-treatment follow-up.
Which implant brands are used in Vietnam?
Premium clinics stock the same globally certified brands used in Australia: Straumann (Switzerland), Nobel Biocare (Sweden), Osstem (South Korea), Dentium (South Korea), and MegaGen (South Korea). Always confirm the brand in writing before treatment.
Can I claim dental work in Vietnam on Australian health insurance?
Some Australian health funds offer limited rebates for overseas dental treatment. Contact your provider before travelling. Even without any rebate, the 70%+ savings make Vietnam significantly more affordable overall.
What if something goes wrong after I return home?
Our partner clinics provide written warranties and will coordinate with a dentist in your home country if needed. Some clinics reimburse reasonable repair costs within the warranty period. We strongly recommend travel insurance that includes dental complication cover.
How do I choose the right clinic in Vietnam?
Look for verified Google reviews (4.5+), international accreditations, dentist credentials from recognized institutions, transparent all-inclusive pricing, English-speaking staff, and warranty policies provided in writing before treatment.
Which cities in Vietnam are best for dental implants?
Ho Chi Minh City is the dental tourism capital with the widest selection of international-standard clinics. Hanoi offers excellent options, especially for complex cases. Da Nang is popular with patients who prefer a quieter coastal setting for recovery.
